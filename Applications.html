Future Uses

Human-Robot Collaboration

Humanoid robots could work alongside humans in various industries, assisting with tasks that are too dangerous, repetitive, or labor-intensive for people, such as in construction or hazardous material handling.

Healthcare Assistance

In the future, humanoid robots could become invaluable companions in healthcare, not only assisting elderly or disabled individuals with daily activities but also monitoring vital signs, administering medications, and offering mental health support through social interaction.

Autonomous Mobility

Humanoid robots could achieve full mobility, moving freely in dynamic environments like homes, workplaces, and public spaces, including navigating stairs, uneven terrain, and interacting with other machines or devices autonomously.
